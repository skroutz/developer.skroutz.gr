About

A SKU (Stock Keeping Unit) is an aggregation of products.

Filtering

You have to specify a category in order to perform filtering. You may supply the optional parameter include_meta[]=available_filters to get meta information about the filters that can be applied.

  • path: /categories/279/skus
  • params:
    • per: 1
    • include_meta: ["available_filters"]
    • oauth_token: [FILTERED]
